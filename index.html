Hardstyle leads, fast.

HyperWave is a supersaw synth for punchy EDM hooks without the clutter.

Why HyperWave

  • Supersaw-style unison oscillator Stacked unison saws with controllable detune, spread, and weight for huge leads in seconds.
  • Mix-ready presence Designed to cut through dense kicks and percussion so your hooks stay upfront in the mix.
  • Fast workflow Minimal controls, instant feedback—dial in width, grit, and movement without menu diving.
  • EDM-first, genre-flexible Perfect for harder styles like hardstyle and rawstyle, while staying versatile for trance, big room, hands-up, and modern EDM.

Sound

HyperWave delivers thick, aggressive stacks with a classic hardware-style unison character—streamlined for producers who need mix-ready leads fast.

Built for producers who move fast

HyperWave keeps the focus on the sound: wide stereo unison, punchy envelopes, and tone shaping that stays upfront when layered with big drums. Create signature leads, plucks, and basslines for festival drops and late-night studio sessions alike.
